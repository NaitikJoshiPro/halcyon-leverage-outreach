The bundle

One employer call.
Two revenue lines.

The bundle is structurally simple. At the close of every Leverage Careers employer conversation — whether that's a Vellore-trained RN going to a Texas primary care practice or an N3-certified caregiver heading to Japan — the rep offers a one-line handoff: "We work with a billing partner called Halcyon. Want a 20-min intro?"

From there, Halcyon takes the meeting, runs the demo, sends the contract, and onboards. Leverage Careers receives 25% of the practice's first-month Halcyon fee on signature and 10% recurring on every monthly invoice for the lifetime of the relationship. No cap. No clawback after month one. Auto-paid via ACH/wire.

We propose two integration depths — pick the one that's least disruptive to your team's existing playbook.

Integration · Light
Email handoff template
Leverage Careers reps send a standard three-way intro email at close of placement — no CRM changes, no new fields. Halcyon's CRM tracks attribution; Leverage gets a monthly statement and a single ACH payout.
Integration · Deep
Co-branded landing page + UTM
A halcyon.leverageedu.com (or similar) sub-page; UTM-tagged outbound; live in the existing partner dashboard. Reports flow to Leverage's existing analytics. Two-week build on Halcyon's side.
Compliance
No PHI flows through Leverage
Leverage is not a Business Associate under HIPAA. No patient data, no BAA required on Leverage's side. The introduction ends at the practice owner; Halcyon's BAA covers everything downstream.

The honest math

Per practice
that signs & stays.

Halcyon doesn't pay channel partners a salary, retainer, or upfront fee. The economics are 100% performance-based — they kick in only once a referred practice signs a contract and pays its first invoice. From there: 25% of the practice's first-month Halcyon fee, paid once. 10% of every subsequent monthly fee, recurring as long as the practice stays. No cap. No expiry.
