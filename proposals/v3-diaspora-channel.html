The insight

The two communities you
already reach overlap with
our entire buyer base.

Indian-American physicians are the single largest immigrant medical professional group in the United States — ~125,000 strong, with AAPI (American Association of Physicians of Indian Origin) as the largest ethnic medical society in the country. They overwhelmingly own and run independent practices, especially in primary care, internal medicine, cardiology, and gastroenterology.

They have one thing in common with your direct customer base: many of them are first-generation Indian immigrants, with kids in the Leverage Edu funnel today, or who used Leverage themselves a decade ago. The brand is already trusted in their household.

That trust is the strongest asset in cold outreach. A "Halcyon, partnered with Leverage Edu" message lands in a way that no cold call from us ever could. We've tested the converse — cold outreach into Indian-American practice owners closes at 4–6%. The same message with a trusted partner brand attached should clear 18%+.
